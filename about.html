About Andrea Aimar

Photojournalist & Documentary Photographer

Professional Journey

Andrea Aimar is an accomplished Italian photojournalist and documentary photographer who has dedicated their career to capturing the human stories behind global conflicts and social issues. With a keen eye for detail and an unwavering commitment to truth-telling, Andrea's work brings light to the world's most pressing humanitarian crises.

Their photographic journey has taken them to conflict zones and areas of political upheaval across the globe, from the war-torn regions of Syria and Ukraine to the challenging terrains of Afghanistan and South Sudan. Through their lens, Andrea captures not just the immediate impact of conflict, but the resilience and humanity that persists even in the darkest circumstances.

Recognition & Publications

Andrea's powerful imagery has been featured in major international publications including The New York Times, The Guardian, Le Monde, Der Spiegel, and numerous other prestigious outlets. Their work has been exhibited in galleries and museums worldwide, bringing crucial attention to forgotten conflicts and underreported stories.
