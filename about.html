About Andrea Aimar

Photojournalist & Documentary Photographer

Professional Journey

Andrea Aimar is an accomplished Italian photojournalist and documentary photographer who has dedicated their career to capturing the human stories behind global conflicts and social issues. With a keen eye for detail and an unwavering commitment to truth-telling, Andrea's work brings light to the world's most pressing humanitarian crises.

Over the past decade, Andrea has covered major conflicts and social movements across the globe, from the aftermath of the Taliban's return in Afghanistan to the ongoing war in Ukraine. Their work has been featured in leading international publications and has earned recognition for its powerful storytelling and ethical approach to conflict photography.

Based between Italy and the field, Andrea continues to document the resilience of communities affected by conflict, always seeking to preserve the dignity of those whose stories they tell.
